Overview

How we handle your information

This Privacy Policy explains how Cleer Money ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and discloses your personal information when you use the Cleer Money mobile application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), and the Consumer Data Right (CDR) framework under the Competition and Consumer Act 2010 (Cth). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Policy.

Data collection

Information we collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you create an account.
  • Subscription information: Billing and subscription status (processed by Apple — we do not store payment card details).

Banking and financial data (via Open Banking / CDR)

With your explicit consent, we connect to your bank accounts via Australia's Consumer Data Right framework through our accredited data recipient partner, Fiskil. Through this connection we may access:

  • Your bank account names and identifiers
  • Transaction history (up to 6 months of historical data)
  • Transaction descriptions, amounts, dates, and merchant information

We access only the data necessary to provide the App's core functionality. We do not access or store your internet banking login credentials.

App usage & technical data

  • Transaction categorisation choices ("Want" / "Need" swipes) and preferences
  • Device type and operating system version (for troubleshooting)
  • Anonymised analytics data to help us understand and improve the App
Data use

How we use your information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App and its features
  • Connect to your bank accounts and retrieve transaction data with your consent
  • Enable you to categorise and analyse your personal spending
  • Send you relevant notifications about your finances (with your permission)
  • Process and manage your subscription via Apple's in-app purchase system
  • Respond to support requests, improve the App, and comply with legal obligations
🔒

We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or on-selling purposes.

Open Banking

Consumer Data Right — your rights

Cleer Money accesses your banking data under Australia's Consumer Data Right framework. In relation to CDR data:

  • You control your consent. Withdraw at any time through the App or your bank's CDR dashboard — no penalty.
  • Data minimisation. We only request the data necessary to operate the App.
  • Data retention. CDR data is deleted within 7 days of account deletion, except where law requires otherwise.
  • No on-selling. Your CDR data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for secondary purposes.
  • Accredited data recipient. Banking data is retrieved via Fiskil Pty Ltd. See fiskil.com.au for their privacy policy.

Security

Data storage & security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure (hosted in Australia where available, otherwise in AWS data centres). Our security measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Encryption of sensitive credentials at rest (Supabase Vault)
  • Row-level security controls on all database tables
  • Access controls limited to authorised systems

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your rights

Access, correction & deletion

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or out-of-date personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data at any time
  • Withdraw CDR consent at any time without affecting your ability to continue using the App
  • Complain if you believe we have mishandled your information

Contact us at support@cleermoney.com.au to exercise any of these rights. We will respond within 30 days. If unsatisfied, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner at oaic.gov.au or 1300 363 992.
