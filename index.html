How it works

Three steps to
financial clarity

No complex setup. No manual entry. Just connect, swipe, and understand your spending like never before.

🏦
1

Connect your bank

Link your Australian bank account securely through Open Banking (CDR). Cleer Money is read-only — we can never move your money.

👆
2

Swipe to categorise

Transactions appear as cards. Swipe right for Need, left for Want. It takes seconds — and builds a picture of your spending habits over time.

📊
3

Get clear insights

See exactly where your money goes — by category, by week, by pattern. Set targets and track whether you're on pace to meet them.
